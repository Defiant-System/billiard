### How to
8-Ball billiards game is played with 15 object balls and the cue ball. One player aims to 
pocket the solids (balls 1–7) and the other the stripes (balls 9–15). After the break, the 
table remains open until a player legally pockets a called ball, which determines their group.
Players continue shooting as long as they legally pocket one of their own balls. Once all seven 
of their group balls are cleared, they must pocket the 8-ball in the called pocket to win. 
Pocketing the 8-ball early or committing a foul on the 8-ball results in a loss.
